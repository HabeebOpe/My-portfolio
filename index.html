About me

Hi, I'm Habeebullah Okunlola, a web developer and a designer passionate about bringing creativity and innovation to projects. I discovered my love for coding in 2021 through a friend and found my niche in problem-solving during HTML practice sessions.

As a designer, what sets me apart is the desire to infuse my design skills into every project. The compliments I receive from friends and fellow developers upon completing projects serve as motivation, driving me to continue building.

Understanding both fields allows for innovative solutions at the interface of biology and technology. Excited about collaborating with like-minded individuals, I'm actively seeking internship opportunities to apply my passion for technology and biotechnology.
